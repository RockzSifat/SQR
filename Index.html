The Story of Square

SQUARE today symbolizes a name — a state of mind. Since the inception in 1958, it has today burgeoned into one of the most successful conglomerates in Bangladesh. The group started business in 1958 with a pharmaceutical venture. Over the next six decades it has become a pioneer in diversified fields of business.

Square Denims Limited

Based in Habiganj, north-eastern Bangladesh, this denim factory was founded in 2015 equipped with cutting-edge machinery to produce a wide range of denim fabrics — making it one of the country's most innovative fabric manufacturers. Square Denims strives for creativity and excellence, achieving it naturally with a touch of heritage.
